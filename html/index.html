What is First Lego League(FLL)?

First Lego League

The FIRST LEGO League (FLL) Challenge is an international robotics competition designed for children aged 9 to 16 years old. The competition combines LEGO Mindstorms, a robotics kit, with real-world engineering and problem-solving challenges. The main goal of FLL is to inspire young participants to develop an interest in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields while fostering teamwork, creativity, and critical thinking.

Robot Game

Teams build and program an autonomous robot using the LEGO Mindstorms kit to complete various missions on a themed playing field. The missions are related to the year's challenge and involve tasks that the robot must accomplish within a specific time limit. Points are awarded based on the successful completion of missions.
